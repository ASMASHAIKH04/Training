HARRY POTTER

BIRTHDAY: JULY 31

Harry Potter is a wizard, the only child of James and Lily Potter. He is famous for having survived an attack by Lord Voldemort when he was a baby. He is also sometimes known as 'The Boy Who Lived'. For the first eleven years of his life Harry lives with his mean aunt and uncle and is unaware of his wizarding roots.

FRIENDS

FIRST DAY OF SCHOOL: SEPTEMBER 1

Throughout his many years at Hogwarts, Harry faces numerous obstacles and perilous adventures besides his two best friends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger. Ronald, better known as Ron Weasley, instantly bonds with Harry at the start of their Hogwarts career.
